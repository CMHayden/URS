What do we do?

We offer consulting services aimed towards the improvement of automated painting processes.


We can commission and program paint robots from a number of brands such as Durr, Abb, or Kuka.


We develop offline programs and deal with their implementation onsite.


Mastery in industry 4.0 and in systems of control and supervision for performance improvements, preventive maintenance and reduction of the ecological footprint.


Help with production and with troubleshooting.


We offer technical teaching.


We can carry out work worldwide, with our extensive portfolio of highly qualified and experienced workers in the sector.
